Company Mission Statement

Salmon cookies are made the old-fashioned way here at Salmon Cookies! How do we make these cookies?! First we fish the salmon out of the water, smash 'em up, smoosh them into cookie dough, batter 'em up, deep fry them, and finally cover them in a sweet sugar glaze! These ain't your grandma's salmon cookies!
